# The Little Red Riding Hood - Text Based Adventure Game

The Little Red Riding Hood game is a text based adventure game that follows the classic folktale story of Little Red Riding Hood.

## Beginning Scene
The opening page features the logo of the game, begin button and credit button that serves as the beginning screen that the user will see.
<img  alt="Beginning page that includes the logo of The Little Red Riding hood with two buttons that say Begin and Credits respectively and a footer that says 'Made by Jayla Craddock in April 2026 for ITSE 2371-001 Front-End Frameworks." src="./littlered/src/assets/beginScreenShot.png" />

## First story scene
The story page follows after the user clicks on the begin button that follows the story of Little Red Riding Hood. In the story it has several different pictures and scenes to keep the user engaged throughout the story.
<img  alt="Story page that has an image of the forest in the morning time with trees, grass and bushes that has two buttons on the top right that say Progress and Restart Story with the dialogue text and the bottom saying 'Once upon a time, there was a girl named Little Red Riding Hood skipping through the forest.' That has a next button that is under the dialogue." src="./littlered/src/assets/forestScreenShot.png" />

## Progress and Restart button
Throughout the entire story includes the progress and restart button that allows the user to check their progress in the story as well as start over from the very beginning of the story.
<img  alt="Progress section popup that says 'Your Progress' that includes: current scene: Forest, Farthest scene reached: Forest, Progress 0/5 scenes and Completion: 0.0% with a close button at the top right. Also includes Restart button that is at the top right of the scene of the forest screen." src="./littlered/src/assets/progressScreenShot.png" />

## Second story scene with choices
The second scene showcases the choices the user can choose from, the choices that the user makes determines the outcome of the story. 
<img  alt="Showcases images of all of the choices you can make during the scene in the Kitchen the image includes: cloud that is in a chat bubble, chess piece, house in a chat bubble, chocolate chip cookie, deer, pencil, The Three Little Bears, Jack and the Beanstalk, paint palette, family in a chat bubble, berry pie, sandwich, tic-tac-toe, poker cards, spade, pottery vase, The boy who cried wolf, broom, The Tortoise and the Hare, dishes, fish, duck and checkers. " src="./littlered/src/assets/choicesScreenShot.png" />

## Third story scene
The third scene shows the outcome of picking the wrong choice in the story that leads to a game over screen that the user can click on the restart adventure button to start over from the beginning.
<img  alt="Black screen with text that says 'Game Over' with an image of a skull that has a restart adventure button at the top right corner." src="./littlered/src/assets/deathScreenShot.png" />
